About Us

For the Love of Flowers

Established in 2016, Floralology, pronounced flow-rul-ology, is a dedicated team of professional florists and botanists who aspire to bring more beauty into the world through the crafting of artisan floral arrangements. We strongly believe that a beautifully crafted bouquet has a powerful voice and effect: fresh blooms and snippets of all kinds of natural fauna arranged to perfection, have the unique ability to bear special significance, lighten one's spirits and create a wonderful atmosphere. At Floralology, we believe that we all have a claim to cherish, protect, and ultimately enjoy nature in all its glory. With our keen love of nature, we continue to share our unique design perspective of shaping unique, meaningful, and quality bouquets and arrangements which lets you enjoy nature and bask in a special taste of its beauty.



We Wake Up Every Day to Share Our Love of Florals

As we love flowers and want to share our love with our customers, we work to present our customers with artisan floral creations infused with meaning. As we continue to grow, we are dedicated to ensuring that our goal continues to further strengthen, expand, and bloom. Please, feel free to contact our team about any questions that you might have about our brand or products.
