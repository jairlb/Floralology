We Have the Flowers For it

Our talented team of florists pride themselves with continuing the long-standing tradition of using flora and fauna to convey human sensibilities, moods and emotions. We employ classical design techniques, including the Victorian language of flowers, to breath special significance into our floral creations. Though our design ethic takes into account the historical considerations of certain stems and old techniques, we also take into account how to mix past significance with modern motifs and design. This ensures that our bouquets contain a unique air of chic modernity, rich history, and meaning. We offer a large selection of bouquets to choose from and we believe that we can offer fresh high-quality floral arrangements perfect for any occasion. Shop from our varied ready-to-ship artisan collections, including our distinctive Wedding Collection. Though we have our own signature design process, we would love to work with you to create artisan custom bouquets with your design input that are made to match any event you find life throwing your way.

How it Works

Visit us at our La Jolla location or order online from one of our collections of artisan bouquets. For online orders, standard shipping is expedient. We ensure that your flowers arrive within a 1 to 2-day window, except during holidays, in pristine condition or we will refund your order. Custom arrangements require at least a 2-day processing time prior to shipment. Certain exceptions can be made depending on the availability of a flower types: if certain plant types are out of season we will not be able to include them in custom orders. In case of any other questions you might have, visit our contact page, send us any question you might have, and we will try to contact you as soon as possible. And did we mention? Shipping is on us! Shipping on all orders, except oversized custom orders, are shipped to you at no cost.
