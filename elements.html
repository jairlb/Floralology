Elements

Project 3 Revisions

Revisions include the addition of JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, another page and edits to the design. The revisions for Project 3 attempted to make the site more akin to contemporary online shopping sites. Changes include making the margins and padding more consistent, making headline fonts decorative, adding dropdown navigation, the addition of an about us page, and another form was implemented throughout all pages for the purpose of being a mock newsletter option. At the recommendation of one of the Project 2 critiques, more self-promoting content in the footer was added. Certain pictures, that seemed to mismatch the brand and design of the site were also excluded. Since the contact page appeared to be a little sparse and less consistent to the design theme, more standard contact information was added, the layout was altered, and the form was spruced to be more monochromatic and adhere to the site's theme. Also, hr elements were added in certain places to section off certain content.
