Custom Collection

Let's Work Together

We would love to work with you to create personal floral arrangements for any event: a single bouquet for a room or a full room of bouquets. Contact our team and send us a bouquet recipe of florals that you want included and we will start working together to make sure that you get perfectly personalized bouquets that say what you want them so say.

How to Get Started

Call us at (858) XXX-XXXX to get started right away or email us. Though email will take longer, because of the amount of time it takes to email back and forth, you will be allowed the same freedom over your creation and will also be assigned to one of our specific florists to help you along the way. When you first email us, or call us, we recommend that you let us know what kind of event the flowers will be for. Also, you can describe what style or aesthetic you want your custom bouquets designed with. Feel free to include any specific flower types or names that you want included in your custom bouquets. After you contact us, we will usually respond within a day to begin planning your custom creation and will start providing certain styling tips and advice until you are absolutely ready to order.

Pricing

Floralology custom bouquets can start at $89.99, but the price can differ depending on the types of flowers used and the number of desired bouquets. Shipping is free on all single bouquets.

Note:

  • If we do not work with certain plant types
  • or, certain flowers are out of season

it is possible that your custom bouquet will not contain such specific flower types. We will try to let you know prior to shipment and allow you the chance to edit your bouquet design.
