About Our Collections

Shop from our ready-to-ship artisan collections of fresh bouquets or let us help you design your own custom bouquet.

Each of our specialty collections are designed with care and fit a certain theme. Our gallery shows a variety of bouquets from our Classics, Emoting and Wedding Collections.

Our Classics collection includes a diverse selection of our favorite bouquets that are as perfect as special gifts as they are decorating a table. Our Emoting collection is our original collection that uses the floral significance and meanings from the Victorian language of flowers to let our bouquets speak for themselves. We offer a range of bouquets with specific meanings that ensure that your flowers say what you want them to say for you. Though we would recommend considering working with us to make a specialized custom bridal bouquet that fits the theme of your nuptials, our Wedding collections offers a variety of bouquets ready to be held in the arms of any bride. We also offer a selection of smaller through larger bridal bouquets and specialty cascading bouquets.
